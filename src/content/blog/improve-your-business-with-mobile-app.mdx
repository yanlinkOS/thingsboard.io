## Your Entire IoT Platform, Now in Your Pocket

Managing an IoT deployment used to mean being chained to a desk.
Whether you were monitoring energy consumption across a facility, tracking the health of field equipment, or watching for alarm threshold breaches, you needed a desktop screen.
**The ThingsBoard mobile app changes that.**

Available on both iOS and Android, <a href="/products/mobile-pe/" target="_blank">the IoT mobile app</a> brings the full power of your ThingsBoard dashboards to your smartphone.
Real-time device data, alarm notifications, and interactive widgets — the same experience your team uses at their desks, now accessible from anywhere.

### Core capabilities of the IoT mobile app
- Monitor live device data and sensor readings in real time
- Receive and manage alarms the moment they trigger, with push notifications
- Interact with the same dashboards your team uses on desktop — no separate setup required
- Control devices and sensors from your phone
- Work in the field with confidence, even in environments with limited connectivity

![The ThingsBoard mobile app](/images/blog/improve-your-business-with-mobile-app/desktop-to-mobile.webp)

## IoT mobile app development - how you can do it
Like the ThingsBoard platform itself, the mobile app is **fully open source**.
The source code is publicly available on <a href="https://github.com/thingsboard/flutter_thingsboard_pe_app" target="_blank">GitHub</a>, so your team can inspect, customize, and build on it.
There is no black box — just clean, extensible code that your developers can adapt to your exact requirements.
For businesses that want to deliver a branded mobile experience without having to start from scratch, this is a significant advantage.

## IoT mobile app development services - Clients' feedback
The best measure of any tool is the **impact** it has on the businesses that use it.
When your field team receives an alarm on their phone the moment a threshold is crossed — rather than discovering it hours later at the office — the window for response shrinks dramatically. 
For businesses where uptime translates directly into revenue or safety, that difference is measurable.

We reached out to clients who have been running the ThingsBoard mobile app in production to understand how it has changed their day-to-day operations — and what it has meant for the end customers they serve.
