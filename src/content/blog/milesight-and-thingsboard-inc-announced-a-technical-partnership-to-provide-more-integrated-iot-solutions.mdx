Xiamen, China, and New York, United States — [Milesight](https://www.milesight.com/), an innovative IoT products provider, and [ThingsBoard](https://www.thingsboard.io/)[, Inc.](https://www.thingsboard.io/), an IoT-related software product vendor, particularly an open-source IoT platform provider, have announced a technical partnership. This collaboration aims to deliver highly integrated IoT solutions to customers. Both companies aim to provide comprehensive and seamless solutions for diverse IoT applications by combining their expertise and technologies.

Milesight, a pioneer in future-oriented IoT technologies encompassing sensing, LoRaWAN®, AI, and 5G, has established itself as a leader by introducing a wide range of cutting-edge products, including highly reliable gateways, indoor air quality sensors, occupancy and people counting sensors, water leak detectors, and many more. Through continuous innovation, Milesight reaffirms its commitment to delivering state-of-the-art products that cater to the evolving needs of the IoT landscape.

> As customers often come to us with a request to recommend hardware vendors, we want to provide them with reliable vendors we vouch for, like Milesight. This partnership reflects the joint dedication of both sides to provide reliable IoT solutions. We are committed to a long-term collaboration that will bring lasting benefits to our customers.

ThingsBoard is an IoT platform for data collection, processing, visualization, and device management. The platform enables device connectivity via industry-standard IoT protocols or stream data from devices connected to existing IoT Platforms. The platform supports both cloud and on-premises deployments, combining scalability, fault tolerance, and performance so that the users’ data remains safe.

In collaboration with ThingsBoard, Milesight has established seamless integration of its gateways and devices with the robust ThingsBoard IoT platform. This partnership aims to provide customers with more integrated IoT solutions that leverage the combined strengths of both companies. By combining Milesight’s innovative gateways and devices with the powerful capabilities of the ThingsBoard IoT platform, users can achieve enhanced connectivity, streamlined device management, and advanced data analytics.

> This partnership shows that Milesight and ThingsBoard are committed to empowering businesses with complete and dependable IoT solutions. By combining both of our technologies, we aim to spark digital transformation, boost operational efficiency, and open up new possibilities in the world of IoT applications.

## About [thingsboard](https://thingsboard.io/)

Established in 2016, ThingsBoard, Inc. specializes in developing IoT software products under the ThingsBoard™ umbrella. Our flagship IoT platform is the most popular open-source project in its class. ThingsBoard’s freeware and licensed software is widely utilized by IoT enthusiasts designing and prototyping smart solutions in their garages and industrial customers with diverse requirements for device management, data processing, security, privacy, analysis, etc.

## About [milesight](https://www.milesight.com/)

Milesight is a fast-growing high-tech company delivering smart IoT and video surveillance products with a focus on IoT technologies, including Artificial Intelligence, 5G, and LoRaWAN®, since 2011. With improved sensing capability and advanced IoT insights, we help customers worldwide optimize their business operations in a more efficient and sustainable way. In collaboration with a global network of distributors and system integrators, we are committed to building open ecosystems and accelerating IoT strategies to the next level.
