ThingsBoard IoT Hub is the marketplace where the ThingsBoard ecosystem discovers, installs, and builds upon your work. When you contribute a device package, solution template, widget, rule chain, calculated field, or alarm rule, it becomes available to thousands of ThingsBoard tenants worldwide — installable in one click.

## Why Contribute?

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Hardware Vendors" icon="server">
    **Global visibility** — showcase your hardware across the ThingsBoard ecosystem and position it as a platform recommendation.

    **A complete digital experience** — ship your device with a ready-to-use dashboard so customers can visualize data from the first connection.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Distributors & System Integrators" icon="external">
    **A pipeline of consulting opportunities** — users who install your components can discover you through your Creator profile and contact you for integration, customization, and consulting services.

    **Showcase your expertise where projects begin** — presence in the IoT Hub puts your work in front of teams actively building on ThingsBoard exactly when they need an implementation partner.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Creators & Affiliates" icon="pencil">
    **Platform presence** — your Creator profile serves as a searchable portfolio for the ThingsBoard community looking for proven solutions.

    **Monetize your expertise** — earn through referral and affiliate links when users discover and install your components.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
