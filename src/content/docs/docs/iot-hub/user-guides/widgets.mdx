**Widgets** is a catalog of pre-configured dashboard components in ThingsBoard's **IoT Hub**, designed for common IoT use cases across different industries. Each widget installs directly into your **Widgets library** — click **Install** and the widget becomes immediately available to add to any dashboard.

## Installing a Widget

To open the catalog, click **IoT Hub** in the ThingsBoard left sidebar and select **Widgets**.

<Steps>
  1. Browse the catalog and find the widget you want.
  2. Click the widget card to open its detail panel and review the description, datasource requirements, and available settings before installing.
  3. Click **Install** — either directly on the card or from inside the detail panel.
  4. Confirm in the dialog. The widget is added to your Widgets library automatically.
  5. When installation completes, click **Open Widget** to navigate directly to the widget in **Resources → Widgets library**, or close and continue browsing.
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
  Installed widgets are available in the widget picker when editing any dashboard — open a dashboard, enter edit mode, click **Add widget**, and select the widget from the library.
</Aside>

## Removing a Widget

Click the **Installed Items** button at the top of the Widgets page to open the list of installed widgets. Click the **Remove** icon next to the widget you want to remove and confirm the action. 

<Aside type="caution">
  This will permanently delete the widget from your tenant.
</Aside>

## Checking for Updates

Click the **Installed Items** button at the top of the Widgets page, then click **Check for updates** in the top-right corner. ThingsBoard compares all installed widgets against the latest versions and adds an **Updates** column showing either **Up to date** or an available version to install.
