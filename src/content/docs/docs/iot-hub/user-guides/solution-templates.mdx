**Solution Templates** is a catalog of complete IoT solution packages in ThingsBoard's **IoT Hub**, built around real-world use cases. Each template provisions a ready-to-use environment in your tenant automatically — click **Install** and get a working IoT solution in seconds.

Depending on the template, the installation creates: <DocLink product={Products.CE} path="user-guide/devices/" target="_blank">Devices (pre-configured with simulated data)</DocLink>, <DocLink product={Products.CE} path="user-guide/device-profiles/" target="_blank">Device Profile</DocLink>, <DocLink product={Products.CE} path="user-guide/dashboards/" target="_blank" target="_blank">Dashboard</DocLink>, <DocLink product={Products.CE} path="user-guide/rule-engine/#rule-chain" target="_blank">Rule Chains</DocLink>, <DocLink product={Products.CE} path="user-guide/alarm-rules/" target="_blank">Alarm Rules</DocLink>, <DocLink product={Products.CE} path="user-guide/calculated-fields/" target="_blank">Calculated Fields</DocLink>, <DocLink product={Products.CE} path="user-guide/customers/" target="_blank">Customers</DocLink>, and <DocLink product={Products.CE} path="user-guide/users/" target="_blank">Users</DocLink>.

## Installing a Solution Template

To open the catalog, click **IoT Hub** in the ThingsBoard left sidebar and select **Solution Templates**.

<Steps>
  1. Browse the catalog. Filter by **Category** or **Use case** in the left panel, or use the search bar. Sort by **Most Installed** to see the most popular templates.
  2. Click a template card to open its detail panel and review the description, included components, and use-case scenarios before installing.
  3. Click **Install** — either directly on the card or from inside the detail panel.
  4. Confirm in the dialog. The platform automatically creates and configures all resources within your tenant.
  5. When installation completes, a **Solution template successfully installed** dialog opens with solution instructions — a guide to the included dashboards, pre-configured alarm rules, demo device credentials, and next steps for connecting real hardware. Read through it before closing.
  6. Click **Go to main dashboard** to open the installed dashboard.
</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  Each template includes pre-configured demo devices with simulated data so you can explore the dashboard behavior and alarm rules immediately, without connecting real hardware first.
</Aside>

## Removing a Solution Template

Click the **Installed Items** button at the top of the Solution Templates page to open the list of installed templates. Click the **Remove** icon next to the template you want to remove and confirm the action. 

<Aside type="caution">
  This will permanently delete all created entities — dashboards, rule chains, devices, and other associated resources — from your tenant.
</Aside>

## Checking for Updates

Click the **Installed Items** button at the top of the Solution Templates page, then click **Check for updates** in the top-right corner. ThingsBoard compares all installed templates against the latest versions and adds an **Updates** column showing either **Up to date** or an available version to install.
